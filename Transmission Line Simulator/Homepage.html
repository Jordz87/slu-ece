Overview

This transmission line simulator aims to provide visualization
of fundamental concepts in transmission line theory.
It is intended to be used by students to better understand the
concepts and as an instructional tool for instructors.
This simulator is broken into three parts: Transmission Line Calculator,
AC Steady State Simulator, and Transient State Simulator.

Guide

To navigate through the different simulators
hover the mouse pointer on the navigation bar
on the upper right side of the screen.
